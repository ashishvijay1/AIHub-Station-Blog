About AI HUB Station

Welcome to AI HUB Station, a premier AI education and training institute located in Kota, Rajasthan. Our mission is to provide cutting-edge training to students, professionals, and corporates in artificial intelligence, data science, machine learning, and emerging technologies. We combine state-of-the-art infrastructure, hands-on learning, and real-world AI projects to empower learners with industry-relevant skills.

Infrastructure

Our AI HUB Station consists of advanced classrooms and labs designed to foster innovation and creativity. Here are some key features:

  • 5-6 modern classrooms, each equipped with smart TVs, projectors, and collaboration tools.
  • A high-performance AI lab with NVIDIA GPUs for AI model development and machine learning.
  • 3D printers for prototyping robotics and AI-driven solutions.
  • Common areas including a reception, student lounge, and administrative offices with high-tech equipment.

Programs Offered

AI HUB Station offers a range of flexible and comprehensive training programs suitable for all skill levels:

  • Weekly Programs - Short-term courses focusing on specific AI skills.
  • Monthly Programs - Intermediate AI training for deeper learning.
  • 6-Month Program - Prime AI Certificate for hands-on experience and project-based learning.
  • 12-Month Program - NGV AI Professional Certificate for advanced AI training and industry-relevant projects.

Technologies We Use

At AI HUB Station, we believe in teaching using the latest technologies to prepare our learners for real-world challenges. Our programs integrate tools and platforms such as:

  • Microsoft Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud for cloud-based AI model development.
  • NVIDIA GPUs for high-performance AI and machine learning simulations.
  • Programming environments like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and VS Code for AI development.
  • Google Classroom and Microsoft Teams for collaborative learning experiences.
